About MGM JNEC PYQ Portal

Mission: Making exam prep simpler by organizing all previous year papers in one place for MGM JNEC students.

  • Fast access to PYQs — no login required.
  • Filter by year, subject, and branch.
  • Includes model answers, stats, and downloads.
  • Reliable, updated, and distraction-free.
Who’s behind this?
A student-led project from MGM's JNEC, Aurangabad — built by students, for students, as part of our own GATE prep journey.
Credibility:
  • Used by 1000+ students (and counting)
  • Powered by Django & PostgreSQL, hosted on Render
  • No ads. No login required. Just PYQs.
Contact / Feedback: Found a bug? Want to request a missing paper? Email us or send feedback.
How it was built: This portal is built with Django, Bootstrap 5, and a lot of student coffee. Open source, fast, and always improving.
